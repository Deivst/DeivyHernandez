Entrepreneurial Journey

Cofounder: TitanDecko

At TitanDecko, our goal was to transform the traditional surface coverings market through strategic product use and technology. We automated internal processes using Python to predict stock demand by integrating economic and socio-demographic variables. This approach allowed us to improve user experience and adjust supply more efficiently.

Cofounder: TuConstruccion.com.co

Born from the belief that the construction sector could be optimized with AI and quantitative analysis, we implemented classification algorithms and indexing to help people decide where to buy or rent real estate. Through comparative analysis and causality studies, we guide customers toward the best cost-benefit opportunities in the market.

Cofounder: Catuh.com

Catuh emerged to provide an AI-based solution that helps individuals save time on repetitive tasks such as document transcription (invoices, certificates). By leveraging AI, we offer clean, ready-to-use data so organizations can focus on what matters: growth.
